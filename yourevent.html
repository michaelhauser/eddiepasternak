Your Event

Tasteful and Appropriate Song Selections

Eddie has developed a well-honed sensibility for the musical requirements of various circumstances. With a broad repertoire ranging from music of the Big Band era to that of the Big Chill era and including songs from a variety of different cultures, he will choose songs specifically for your venue or special occasion.

Performances Tailored to Your Event

Eddie works in a variety of ensembles made up of the finest local musicians. Whether performing as a solo guitarist, as a part of a duo, or with a larger group, Eddie will create a sound that enhances your environment and delights your listeners.
